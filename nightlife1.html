Event Overview

Step into Accra’s most vibrant nightlife experience at Republic Bar & Grill in Osu. Enjoy premium cocktails, live DJ sets, curated playlists, networking, and a high-energy social atmosphere with some of the best vibes in the city.

Nightlife Experience

  • Top Ghanaian DJs and live music
  • Signature cocktails and premium drinks
  • Curated social mixer and networking segments
  • Exclusive VIP lounge access (limited)
  • Photobooth and nightlife photography

What to Expect

  • Premium nightlife atmosphere in the heart of Osu
  • Curated crowd – fun, friendly and outgoing
  • Great music selections across Afrobeats, Amapiano & RnB
  • Memorable moments captured all night long

Dress Code

Smart casual / night-out chic – no slippers or beachwear. Come ready to take photos!

Entry & Check-In

Doors open at 8:00 PM. Please arrive early to avoid long queues. Show your digital ticket or confirmation code at the entrance for verification.

Refund Policy

Tickets are non-refundable. In case of event cancellation or rescheduling, communication will be shared and refunds processed within 7 working days where applicable.

Safety Guidelines

  • No weapons or dangerous objects allowed.
  • Security checks will be conducted at the entrance.
  • Respect all guests and staff at all times.
  • Drink responsibly. Management reserves the right to deny entry.
